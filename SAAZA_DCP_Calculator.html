DCP Calculator

Dynamic Cone Penetrometer data reduction — blows to CBR, layers, and the controlling (weakest) strength

Enter penetration readings (blows + depth) and this computes the DCP index → CBR for each interval, builds the depth-vs-CBR profile, groups layers, and flags the controlling (weakest) layer. CBR uses TSPWG 3-260-03.02-19 Tables 2-3/2-4. Export to the official DCP Data Sheet (Fig 2-13) or a data file. Your entries save automatically and only clear when you say so.
