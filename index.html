Survey Field Guide New
A step-by-step runner that keeps a zone survey organized and delegated start to finish — for any surveyor, first survey or fiftieth. Covers all three survey types — ALZ (AF 3822), HLZ (AF 4303, with an optional ground-bearing DCP spot-check you switch on for soft spots), and DZ (AF 3823) — and rebuilds the right crews for each (ALZ has a DCP team; HLZ/DZ don't). Enter your team size and it auto-builds the crews; add more survey teams, name members (teams show member names), and move people or hand individual tasks between teams for full flexibility. A per-team equipment table shows exactly what each crew grabs from the truck. Every step shows what to do, who's on it, and links to the governing publication, the exact class slide, and the app tool to use (right down to the tab). Absolute links survive a shared PDF, and the TL can share a locked, red-then-green read-only link/QR that opens the guide with the teams pre-built. In the field: focus to one team's tasks, check off steps, collapsible phases, and a rough time estimate. Print/save as PDF.

Drop Zone Size & PI Calculator New
Minimum DZ dimensions & Point of Impact via MANFAS — or flip it: enter your DZ size to see the max CDS / Personnel / HE it supports (day/night, formation). All numbers from DAFMAN 13-217, shows every step

DCP Calculator New
Blows → CBR → layers → controlling strength. Multi-hole, depth/CBR graph, exports the official DCP Data Sheet. TSPWG Tables 2-3/2-4.
📍
DCP Test Locations New
Where to place DCP readings on an LZ — full vs. hasty survey toggle, plus the test procedure. ETL 97-9 / TSPWG.
🧭
Coordinate Converter New
Convert between Lat/Long, DMS, UTM (Easting/Northing) & MGRS — enter any format, get the rest, incl. deg-dec-min for the survey form. Also converts True/Grid/Magnetic azimuths (auto grid convergence). WGS 84.
📈
Gradient & Glideslope Calculator New
Grade/elevation/angle from clinometer + laser, the 20′ line-level reduction, longitudinal profile (±3% & ±1.5%/200′), transverse gradient (±0.5–3% from CL), Zone A/B drop check (C-130 2′9″/6′3″), and glideslope clearance (C-130 35:1, C-17 20:1). Clinometer % or degrees throughout, field tips on every tab. Unit 1 + DAFMAN 13-217 Tbl 4.2, shows the work.
