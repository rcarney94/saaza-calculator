Survey Field Guide

Step-by-step, in order, for running a zone survey — what to do, the gear each team needs, who you've put on it, and the reference behind it. Keeps the whole survey organized and delegated for any Team Lead — first survey or fiftieth.

How to use this — tap to expand
  1. Set your team size. The tool auto-builds a Team Lead, a DCP team, and a survey/measure team, and assigns everyone.
  2. Need to split the work? + Add DCP team / + Add Survey team creates another team (e.g. two DCP teams of 3). Move anyone with the dropdown next to their name — including Assist TL to put a hand on the Team Lead's tasks.
  3. Each task carries a team dropdown — hand any task to a specific team (give gradients to your strongest crew). The DCP holes split across all DCP teams by default.
  4. The equipment table shows exactly what each team must grab — check it before anyone leaves the truck.
  5. Each task links the app tool to use for it (and the exact tab) — e.g. the DCP team gets DCP Test Locations + DCP Calculator, a gradients crew gets the Gradient Calculator on the right tab. Each team's tools are also rolled up under "What each team needs."
  6. Print / Save as PDF captures the whole thing, teams and all.
  7. Hand-off: the QR card on this page (and on the printout) opens this same live guide for any teammate — references and slide decks included.
  8. In the field: tap Focus to show only one team's tasks (great for a new teammate), check off each step as you finish it (saved on your device), and tap a phase header to collapse it.
Hand this guide to your team

Scan the code (or open the link) and each teammate gets this exact live guide on their own phone — every reference and slide link works. Sharing a PDF instead? Those links still open as long as there's a signal, and this QR jumps anyone to the live version.
